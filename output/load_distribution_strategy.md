# SOFMC Concession Load Distribution Strategy

## The Core Problem

Island Canteen handles **40.5%** of all arena volume (116,428 items) while the other 5 stands split the remaining 60%. This creates long wait times, lost sales, and frustrated fans — especially during intermissions.

---

## The Overlap Reality

**The stands are NOT very differentiated.** Most items are sold at multiple locations, but fans don't know that — or don't have a reason to walk to a less-busy stand.

## Concrete Load Distribution Strategies

### Strategy 1: "Drinks & Snacks Express" Lane at Canteen
**Problem it solves:** 95.5% of Canteen volume is shared items. Many fans in the Canteen line are buying ONLY a beer and popcorn.

**Implementation:**
- Split Canteen into 2 lines: **FOOD** (burgers, fries, tenders, combos) and **GRAB & GO** (beer, pop, popcorn, candy, water)
- The Grab & Go line moves 3-4× faster since no prep is needed
- Estimated impact: Redistributes ~30-40% of Canteen transactions to a faster lane

### Strategy 2: Signage & Digital Wayfinding — "Same Items, Shorter Lines"
**Problem it solves:** Fans don't realize they can get beer, popcorn, and pop at 4-5 other stands.

**Implementation:**
- Large signs near Canteen: *"Skip the line — Beer, Popcorn & Pop also at Portable Stations, Island Slice, Phillips Bar"*
- Arena app: Show real-time estimated wait per stand
- QR codes on seat cup holders linking to a stand map
- Estimated impact: 10-20% reduction in Canteen volume for shared items

### Strategy 3: Add Fries to a Second Stand
**Problem it solves:** Fries are 90% concentrated at Canteen (11,640 units). This is the #1 draw — fans go for fries and buy everything else while waiting.

**Implementation:**
- Add a fryer to **TacoTacoTaco** (already has kitchen infrastructure and only has 10% of fries currently)
- Or add fries to **ReMax Fan Deck** (already serves hot dogs and paninis)
- Estimated impact: Could shift 3,000-5,000 fry orders away from Canteen (15-20% load reduction)

### Strategy 4: Pre-Order for Prep-Heavy Items
**Problem it solves:** Burgers, tenders, and combos require cook time, creating the longest waits.

**Implementation:**
- Mobile order for: Burgers, Chicken Tenders, COMBOS, Fries/Poutine
- Order during Period 1 → pickup at Intermission 1
- Order during Period 2 → pickup at Intermission 2
- Dedicated pickup window at Canteen (no waiting in the regular line)
- Estimated impact: 15-25% reduction in Canteen queue time during intermissions

### Strategy 5: "Roaming Vendors" for Top Grab & Go Items
**Problem it solves:** The top grab-and-go items (beer, pop, water, popcorn) don't need a stand at all.

**Implementation:**
- Hawker-style vendors walking the stands selling:
  - Cans of Beer (35,440 total — the #1 item overall)
  - Bottle Pop (33,546 total)
  - Water (22,592 total)
  - Popcorn (26,404 total)
- Fans buy without leaving their seats = zero impact on stand lines
- Estimated impact: Could absorb 5-10% of total arena volume

### Strategy 6: Make Phillips Bar & Portable Stations More Visible
**Problem it solves:** These two stands sell 100% duplicated items but handle only 8.4% and 13.6% of volume respectively. They're underutilized.

**Implementation:**
- Phillips Bar has capacity for more — it's a craft beer bar but only doing 24K items
- Better wayfinding signage from the Canteen area pointing to Portable Stations
- Consider adding popcorn and cotton candy to Portable Stations (currently 10 and 361 units respectively — essentially non-existent)
- Estimated impact: 5-10% shift in beer/beverage sales from Canteen and ReMax

### Strategy 7: Bundle Incentives at Underused Stands
**Problem it solves:** Even if fans know other stands exist, they default to Canteen because it has everything.

**Implementation:**
- Offer stand-specific combos: "Phillips Combo: Draft Beer + Pretzel for $X"
- TacoTacoTaco combo: "Tacos + Slushy for $X" 
- Island Slice combo: "2 Slices + Pop for $X"
- People love deals → gives fans a reason to visit an alternative stand
- Estimated impact: 5-10% volume shift if priced attractively

## The Bottom Line

**The stands already sell mostly the same items — fans just don't behave that way.** 95% of Canteen's volume is things you can buy at 2-5 other stands. The issue isn't menu differentiation, it's:

1. **Habit** — fans go where they always go
2. **Fries/Burgers/Tenders lock-in** — the 3 exclusive items draw fans in, then they buy everything else while there
3. **No information** — fans don't know which stands are less busy or what they sell
4. **No incentive** — there's no reason to walk to Portable Stations vs. wait at Canteen

Fix those four things and load naturally redistributes without changing a single menu item.
