# Why Island Canteen Handles 40.5% of All Arena Volume

## Summary

Island Canteen isn't just the most popular stand — it's the **only full-service kitchen** in the arena. It sells 116,428 items across all games, nearly 3× the next-highest stand. Every other stand is either a specialty counter, a beverage cart, or a bar. Fans who want a "real meal" have no alternative.

## 7. The Root Cause

Island Canteen's dominance comes from three compounding factors:

1. **Monopoly on core foods**: Burgers, fries, tenders, and poutine — the most fundamental arena foods — are exclusive to Canteen. Fans who want a meal *must* go there.

2. **One-stop-shop effect**: Since fans are already in line for food, they buy their drinks, snacks, and sweets at the same time. This inflates Canteen's share of non-food categories (51% of all Bottle Pop, 47% of all Popcorn).

3. **Combo meals drive multi-item transactions**: COMBOS (Tenders & Fries, Burger & Fries, Popcorn & 2 Dogs) account for 9,008 units — 84% at Canteen. Each combo represents a multi-item basket that would otherwise be split across stands.

---

## 8. Operational Recommendations

### Immediate Actions
1. **Pre-order system priority**: Target Burgers, Fries, Tenders, and Combos — the highest-prep-time items that create the longest lines at Canteen specifically.

2. **Distribute Fries to a second stand**: Fries are 90% concentrated at Canteen (11,640 units). Adding a fryer to Island Slice or the Portable Stations would immediately reduce Canteen load by ~15-20%.

3. **Express beverage lane**: 51% of all Bottle Pop and 47% of all Popcorn are sold alongside food orders at Canteen. A dedicated "drinks & snacks only" register would let quick-purchase fans bypass the food line.

### Strategic Actions
4. **Add hot food at ReMax Fan Deck**: Currently only 7.5% food. Adding burgers or tenders here would create a second full-service option and redistribute demand.

5. **Expand TacoTacoTaco's menu**: Already has kitchen infrastructure (tacos, jalapeño poppers). Adding fries/poutine here would give the arena a second prep kitchen.

6. **Per-stand queue time displays**: Show real-time wait estimates so fans can make informed decisions — many may not realize Phillips Bar or Portable Stations have shorter lines for beer and snacks.
